Teaching and Learning at St Edmund’s

Our 6 C’s below are the core of teaching and learning at St Edmund’s College. We are committed to developing students into life-long learners and through the 6 C’s we develop students who can think critically and engage with the world around them.

The 6 C's:

Create

We create a learning environment that encourages experimentation and exploration of ideas.

Challenge

We challenge ourselves to expand on our ideas and our own learning. We see potential barriers as opportunities for innovation.

Collaborate

We collaborate with others to provide a learning environment of excellence.

Contemplate

We contemplate our place in the world and encourage students to approach all subject areas with a sense of wonder.

Connect

We connect with each other and with the world by personal relationships and via emerging technologies.

Celebrate

We celebrate our successes, our discoveries and our achievements.
