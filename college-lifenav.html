"We are brothers within these walls."

At St Edmund's College, students are expected to activate to their best of their ability within College Life. The school provides a a widerange of well-bring services, to co-currciulars and amazing facilities for students & staff.

Student Wellbeing:

The College has high standards of well-being for their students. There are many programs implemented within the College to make sure students feel safe adn recieve the best education possible.

College Houses

Their are 6 College Houses, in which students are split into upon enrolment. For High School, they are split into 6 House Groups and then tutor groups with students from all Year groups from 7-12 where relationships with elder and other students can be fostered.

Defence Students

The College offers support and assistance to students coming from defence families.

Gifted & Talented

The College offers a wide range of programs for Gifted & Talented students.

Cocurricular:

The College offers a wide range of extra-curricular activities for students and students are expected to participate in 1 per year.

Sports:

There are a wide range of sporting cocurriculars ranging from Rugby to Soccer to Waterpolo

Technology-based:

For the technology interested students, the College provides STEAM where students can enjoy and create inventions.

Arts & Creative

The College provides opportunites for dance, performance and photography based activities interested students.

Facilities:

College Canteen:

The College canteen offers a wide range of food for students.

Chapel:

The College chapel offers a place for students to reflect and connect with God.

Library

The College offers a Junior and Senior school library. The libraries have the latest technology and offers new books and excellent sources for research.
